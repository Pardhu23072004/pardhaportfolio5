04 / projects & experience

Work That Matters

Academic Project IIPE Visakhapatnam

Controller Development Using System Identification in MATLAB

Designed and implemented a feedback control system by identifying the dynamic model of a process using system identification techniques in MATLAB. Applied the System Identification Toolbox to estimate transfer function and state-space models, validated accuracy against measured responses, then tuned PID and model-based controllers to meet performance criteria.

Key outcome: Demonstrated that model-based controller design from real process data significantly outperforms conventional trial-and-error tuning in stability and response speed.
MATLAB System Identification PID Control Transfer Functions Simulink
Academic Project IIPE Visakhapatnam

Simulation of HMF Separation Techniques in Aspen Plus

Modelled and simulated multiple separation processes for the purification of 5-Hydroxymethylfurfural (HMF) using Aspen Plus. Evaluated liquid-liquid extraction, reactive distillation, and vacuum evaporation, comparing energy consumption, separation efficiency, and product purity across configurations using NRTL and UNIQUAC property methods.

Key outcome: Identified the most energy-efficient separation route for HMF with >95% purity, contributing to the techno-economic feasibility of bio-refinery processes.
Aspen Plus HMF Separation Processes Thermodynamics Biorefinery
Summer Internship HPCL — Operations Dept.

Preheat Maximization of Crude Distillation Unit 4 (CDU-4)

Worked with Operations at Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited on a heat integration study for CDU-4. Analysed the existing heat exchanger network to identify thermal bottlenecks and fouling impacts. Applied pinch analysis principles to evaluate potential network modifications and proposed operational changes to maximise crude inlet temperature.

Key outcome: Identified actionable optimisation opportunities that could reduce fired heater duty and lower fuel gas consumption — translating to cost savings and reduced emissions at industrial scale.
Heat Integration Pinch Analysis CDU Operations Energy Efficiency HPCL
